🗣️ Oral drill: say the full sentence

Read the cue out loud, then she says the full sentence with the correct "going to" form. Flip only to check.

🗣️ Oral drill: subject swap chain

Say the sentence, then say it again with the new person — out loud, back and forth, no pausing.

  1. "I'm going to visit Ireland." → (she) → "She's going to visit Ireland."
  2. "We're going to stay for two weeks." → (they) → "They're going to stay for two weeks."
  3. "He's going to cook dinner." → (we) → "We're going to cook dinner."
  4. "She's going to meet us at the airport." → (he) → "He's going to meet us at the airport."
  5. "They're going to love Dublin." → (you) → "You're going to love Dublin."
