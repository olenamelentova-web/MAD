Lesson 06 · 60 min

Future Plans — What Are You Doing Tomorrow?

Talk about upcoming plans — including her actual next trip to Ireland, which makes this immediately useful.

Warm-up

"What are you doing this weekend?"

Vocabulary

Time expressions (tomorrow, next week, next year), travel-planning words.

Grammar

going to + verb, present continuous for fixed plans ("I'm flying on…").

Speaking practice
  • Calendar on Miro — she fills in a week of plans and talks through it
  • "Plan an Ireland trip" — she describes what she'll do when she visits her daughter
Roleplay

Telling the boyfriend about her upcoming visit — dates, what she wants to do there.

Homework

Describe her real plans for her next Ireland trip, spoken.

✦ Backup activities — if time remains
  • Quick round robin: "What are your plans for the weekend?"
  • Packing list — name items she'll bring for the trip
  • Comparing planned vs. wished-for activities: "I'm going to… / I'd like to…"
  • Very light "What will you do if it rains?" — just for exposure
