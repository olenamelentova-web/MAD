Lesson 01 · 60 min

Introduction & Getting to Know You

Build confidence speaking about herself; establish "always answer in a full sentence" from day one.

Warm-up

Name, where she's from, one thing about her day — modeled by you first.

Vocabulary

Personal info words: name, age, from, live, work / retired, family.

Grammar

to be + simple present — "I am…", "I live in…", "I have…"

Speaking practice
  • Question drill: you ask, she answers, then she asks you back
  • "Interview" cards on Miro — she picks a card, answers, no translating pauses (gentle timer)
Roleplay

Meeting her daughter's boyfriend for the first time — introducing herself, small polite exchange.

Homework

Record a 30-second voice note introducing herself.

✦ Backup activities — if time remains
  • Spell her name, email and phone number out loud
  • "Two truths and a lie" about herself
  • Numbers drill: age, address, phone number — said fast
  • Simple "20 questions" — she asks yes/no questions to guess a job or place
