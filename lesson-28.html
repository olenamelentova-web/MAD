🗣️ Oral drill: swap chain

Say the direction, then say it again swapping in the new word — out loud, back and forth, no pausing.

  1. "Turn left at the corner." → (right) → "Turn right at the corner."
  2. "It's opposite the church." → (near) → "It's near the church."
  3. "Go straight for two minutes." → (five) → "Go straight for five minutes."
  4. "It's next to the market." → (behind) → "It's behind the market."
  5. "You can't miss it." → (won't) → "You won't miss it."
