🗣️ Oral drill: swap chain

Say the line, then say it again swapping in the new word — out loud, back and forth, no pausing.

  1. "Shall we meet on Saturday?" → (Sunday) → "Shall we meet on Sunday?"
  2. "Are you free on Sunday?" → (Monday) → "Are you free on Monday?"
  3. "Can you make it at 6pm?" → (7pm) → "Can you make it at 7pm?"
  4. "Would you like to come for dinner?" → (lunch) → "Would you like to come for lunch?"
  5. "Let's find another time." → (place) → "Let's find another place."
