🗣️ Oral drill: swap chain

Say the sentence, then say it again swapping in the new word — out loud, back and forth, no pausing.

  1. "I enjoy reading." → (cooking) → "I enjoy cooking."
  2. "She likes gardening." → (he) → "He likes gardening."
  3. "We love walking." → (swimming) → "We love swimming."
  4. "I'm interested in dancing." → (painting) → "I'm interested in painting."
  5. "They enjoy cycling." → (I) → "I enjoy cycling."
