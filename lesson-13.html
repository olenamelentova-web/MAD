🗣️ Oral drill: swap chain

Say the question, then say it again swapping in the new word — out loud, back and forth, no pausing.

  1. "Can you recommend something for a cough?" → (a headache) → "Can you recommend something for a headache?"
  2. "Do I need a prescription?" → (an appointment) → "Do I need an appointment?"
  3. "How many times a day?" → (a week) → "How many times a week?"
  4. "Can I make an appointment?" → (a call) → "Can I make a call?"
  5. "Is this safe?" → (open) → "Is this open?"
