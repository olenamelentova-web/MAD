🧩 Grammar warm-up: instructions

10 min

Tech instructions use the base form of the verb with no subject ("Open the app," not "You open the app") — a small but important pattern.

🗣️ Oral drill: give the instruction

Read the cue out loud, then she gives the full instruction. Flip only to check.

🗣️ Oral drill: swap chain

Say the instruction, then say it again swapping in the new word — out loud, back and forth, no pausing.

  1. "Open the app." → (close) → "Close the app."
  2. "Click here." → (tap) → "Tap here."
  3. "Don't forget to save it." → (send) → "Don't forget to send it."
  4. "How do I download this?" → (update) → "How do I update this?"
  5. "Enter your password." → (email) → "Enter your email."
