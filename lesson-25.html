🗣️ Oral drill: swap chain

Say the sentence, then say it again swapping in the new word — out loud, back and forth, no pausing.

  1. "I've lost my luggage." → (passport) → "I've lost my passport."
  2. "My flight is delayed." → (cancelled) → "My flight is cancelled."
  3. "The gate has changed." → (time) → "The time has changed."
  4. "I've missed my connection." → (flight) → "I've missed my flight."
  5. "Where's the lost property desk?" → (customs) → "Where's customs?"
