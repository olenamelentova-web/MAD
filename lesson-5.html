Lesson 05 · 60 min

Past Simple — What Did You Do Yesterday?

Comfortably talk about recent past events — key for catching up with the boyfriend or daughter about her trip or daily life.

Warm-up

"What did you have for breakfast?"

Vocabulary

Common daily-routine verbs: woke up, went, ate, met, watched, worked.

Grammar

Past simple, regular/irregular — 10–12 high-frequency irregulars only.

Speaking practice
  • Yesterday timeline on Miro — she places sticky notes in order, narrates her day
  • Chain story: you say one sentence about yesterday, she continues, back and forth
Roleplay

Boyfriend calls and asks "How was your day?" — she gives a full spoken account.

Homework

Voice note: "What I did yesterday" (5+ sentences).

✦ Backup activities — if time remains
  • Compare yesterday vs. last week
  • Describe a memorable past day (e.g. her last Ireland trip)
  • Guess-the-action: mime or describe a past action
  • Light intro to "Have you ever…?" — just for exposure
