Lesson 07 · 60 min

Travel & Getting Around

Airport and transport English — lower frequency but high-stakes once a year.

Warm-up

"What was the last airport you were in like?"

Vocabulary

Airport words (boarding pass, gate, luggage, security), transport (bus, taxi, ticket).

Grammar

Can you tell me…? / How do I get to…? — polite requests and directions.

Speaking practice
  • Airport Miro map — navigate from check-in to gate, asking for help along the way
  • Directions drill: left, right, straight on, near, next to — on a Miro street map
Roleplay

Lost at the airport / asking a stranger for directions to the daughter's house.

Homework

Practice asking "How do I get to…?" with 3 different destinations.

✦ Backup activities — if time remains
  • Reading flight times and gate numbers aloud
  • Roleplay: giving an address to a taxi driver
  • "What's wrong?" — lost luggage, missed bus, wrong platform
  • Reverse roles: she gives directions to you on the Miro map
