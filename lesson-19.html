🗣️ Oral drill: swap chain

Say the question, then say it again swapping in the new word — out loud, back and forth, no pausing.

  1. "How much does this cost?" → (that) → "How much does that cost?"
  2. "Can I pay by card?" → (cash) → "Can I pay by cash?"
  3. "Where's the nearest ATM?" → (bank) → "Where's the nearest bank?"
  4. "Do you accept cash?" → (cards) → "Do you accept cards?"
  5. "Can I get a refund?" → (a receipt) → "Can I get a receipt?"
