Phone Calls & Texting

The language she needs for calling, video-calling, and texting — exactly how she keeps in touch with the boyfriend and Ann between visits. Grammar focus: polite phone requests (Can I speak to…?, I'll call you back), used throughout every task below.

🗣️ Oral drill: swap chain

Say the line, then say it again swapping in the new word — out loud, back and forth, no pausing.

  1. "Can I speak to Ann?" → (the doctor) → "Can I speak to the doctor?"
  2. "Can you hear me?" → (see me) → "Can you see me?"
  3. "I'll call you back later." → (tomorrow) → "I'll call you back tomorrow."
  4. "Is this a good time?" → (tonight) → "Is tonight a good time?"
  5. "I'll text you the details." → (send you) → "I'll send you the details."
