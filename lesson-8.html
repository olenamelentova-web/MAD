Lesson 08 · 60 min

Small Talk & Weather

The "glue" language — casual chit-chat so she can talk with the boyfriend beyond just answering direct questions.

Warm-up

"How's the weather today?"

Vocabulary

Weather words, small-talk starters (How's it going? / Nice, isn't it? / Busy day?).

Grammar

Simple present for general statements, It's + adjective.

Speaking practice
  • Small-talk cards on Miro — random prompts, she responds and asks something back
  • "Keep it going" — practice adding a follow-up question every time
Roleplay

Casual chat with the boyfriend while waiting for the daughter — weather, flight, general chit-chat.

Homework

Have a 2-minute "chat" with a language app or family member, small talk only.

✦ Backup activities — if time remains
  • Compliments and responses: "I like your…" / "Thank you!"
  • Comparing weather back home vs. in Ireland
  • Reacting naturally to news: "Really?" / "That's great!"
  • Practicing polite conversation exits: "See you later"
