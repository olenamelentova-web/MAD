🗣️ Oral drill: swap chain

Say the line, then say it again swapping in the new word — out loud, back and forth, no pausing.

  1. "It's sunny today." → (cold) → "It's cold today."
  2. "How was your weekend?" → (day) → "How was your day?"
  3. "I'm feeling tired." → (relaxed) → "I'm feeling relaxed."
  4. "Nice weather, isn't it?" → (warm) → "Nice and warm, isn't it?"
  5. "See you later!" → (tomorrow) → "See you tomorrow!"
