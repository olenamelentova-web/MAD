🗣️ Oral drill: swap chain

Say the sentence, then say it again swapping in the new word — out loud, back and forth, no pausing.

  1. "Have you ever been to Ireland?" → (Spain) → "Have you ever been to Spain?"
  2. "I have seen that film." → (read that book) → "I have read that book."
  3. "She has never tried sushi." → (he) → "He has never tried sushi."
  4. "We have visited Dublin twice." → (three times) → "We have visited Dublin three times."
  5. "I have never flown before." → (driven) → "I have never driven before."
