Lesson 03 · 60 min

Food & Eating Out

Talk about food preferences and manage a restaurant / café situation.

Warm-up

"What did you eat today?" — a light preview of past tense, no pressure yet.

Vocabulary

Food items, meals, taste words (delicious, too spicy, I don't like…), ordering phrases.

Grammar

like / don't like + noun/gerund, would like for polite requests.

Speaking practice
  • Menu on Miro — she orders from you (waiter roleplay), swap roles
  • "This or that" rapid-fire: coffee or tea? sweet or savory?
Roleplay

Café with the boyfriend — ordering, saying what she likes/dislikes, asking "what's this?"

Homework

Note 5 foods she likes/dislikes in English.

✦ Backup activities — if time remains
  • Describe a simple recipe step by step
  • "Would you like…?" offering / accepting / declining drill
  • Quick food survey — she asks you 5 questions
  • Roleplay: complaining politely in a restaurant
