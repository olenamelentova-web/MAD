🗣️ Oral drill: say the full sentence

Read the cue out loud, then she says the full sentence with correct have/has — no writing, no flipping until she's answered. Flip only to check.

🗣️ Oral drill: possessive swap chain

Say the sentence, then say it again with the new person in brackets — out loud, back and forth, no pausing to think between them.

  1. "This is my daughter." → (he) → "This is his daughter."
  2. "This is her boyfriend." → (they) → "This is their boyfriend."
  3. "This is our house." → (I) → "This is my house."
  4. "This is his family." → (she) → "This is her family."
  5. "This is your daughter." → (we) → "This is our daughter."
