🗣️ Oral drill: subject swap chain

Say the sentence, then say it again with the new person — out loud, back and forth, no pausing.

  1. "I always wake up early." → (she) → "She always wakes up early."
  2. "He usually has tea." → (they) → "They usually have tea."
  3. "We sometimes go out." → (I) → "I sometimes go out."
  4. "She never eats meat." → (we) → "We never eat meat."
  5. "They often visit." → (he) → "He often visits."
