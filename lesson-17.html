Lesson 17 · 90 min · Unit 2

Giving Opinions

Sharing what she thinks, and agreeing or disagreeing without sounding rude. Grammar focus: I think…, In my opinion…, used throughout every task below.

🗣️ Speaking practice

15 min
Opinion swap

Give her a statement — she reacts with agreement or disagreement, plus a reason.

Debate lite

Discuss simple, low-stakes topics — tea vs. coffee, city vs. countryside — exchanging opinions back and forth.

Prompt cards — she answers out loud, flip to check a sample

10 min
