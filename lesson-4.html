Lesson 04 · 60 min

Shopping & Supermarket Survival English

The practical, high-frequency phrases she needs for supermarket trips — small phrase set, drilled until automatic.

Warm-up

"Where do you usually buy…?"

Vocabulary

Shop sections (dairy, bakery, frozen), common items, checkout words (self-checkout, card/cash, receipt, bag).

Grammar

Where is / are…?, Do you have…?, How much is…?

Speaking practice
  • Core phrase drill: "Excuse me, where can I find…?" / "Do you have any…?" / "How much is this?" / "Is this on sale?"
  • Miro supermarket map — she "walks" through it, asking you (playing staff) where things are
Roleplay

Full supermarket trip — find 3 items, ask a question, pay.

Homework

Practice the 4 core phrases out loud, 10 times each.

✦ Backup activities — if time remains
  • Prices and totals drill — add up a small shopping list
  • "Is it fresh?" — asking about expiry dates and substitutions
  • Comparing products: "this one is cheaper / bigger"
  • Self-checkout troubleshooting: "It's not scanning", "I need help, please"
